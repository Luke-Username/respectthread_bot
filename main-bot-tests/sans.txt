Can Goku no-hit Sans?

Goku is not allowed to attack, hakai, or otherwise attempt to harm Sans, fight is in the Judgement Hall. Goku wins if Sans tires out and Sans wins if he hits Goku once.
R1: Base Goku
R2: All SSJ transformations are allowed up to SSJ 3
R3: SSJ God and Blue are allowed
R4: Mastered Ultra Instinct
Bonus: Goku is playing Undertale and has 10 hours to beat Sans using the game systems.
